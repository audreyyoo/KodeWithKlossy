The Other Side


While technology has created challenges in the art world, it has also opened new doors for artists and consumers alike.




The Positive Aspects


Here are some ways in which technology has improved the art world...



OUTREACH


Art organizations are able to bring more attention to their events due to the outreach that technology gives art. This makes it easier for them to fundraise and engage with their audience.



DIVERSITY


The art community has become more diverse due to easy access on the Internet and other technological mediums. This allows art to evolve, as people from different backgrounds are able to be included in consumption and creation.



ACCESSIBILITY


Digital art is much cheaper than physical art, due to only requiring a digital device for creation, rather than buying multiple art supplies. Physical art supplies deteriorate overtime, especially at faster rates than digital devices.
