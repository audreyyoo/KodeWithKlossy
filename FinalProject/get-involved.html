How To Support An Artist





Ways To Support


3 ways YOU can support artists



COMMISSIONING ART


The oldest and most simple way to support artists is by commissioning their art. Commissioning art is simply hiring an artist to make a custom piece for you. This helps artists make a living, but be sure to pay them fairly for their time and effort!



PROMOTION


An even simpler way to help is promoting your favorite artist on social media. Crediting is important and can go a long way for an artist, because it gives the artist more exposure. More exposure means more money and business opportunities. So, next time you see an artist's post, just leave a like and share it!



PAYWALL COMMUNITIES


There are multiple apps and websites that allow artists to put a pay feature on their account. Patreon would be an example of this. This site allows users to pay a membership fee to access art. This decreases art theft and helps puts a steady income in artists' pockets.
